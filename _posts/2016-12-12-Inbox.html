About Inbox

Inbox is a chatrooms app where you can talk about anything you want to with friends or the general public. You can explore and join chatrooms about any topic and start chatting instantly, or create your own chatroom and start a conversation as friends and new people join in. Whether you want to debate a trending news topic, organize a night out or meet new people around you.
